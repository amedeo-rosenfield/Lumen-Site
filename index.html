About

We think lamps have looked the same for too long.

Lumen started with one question: why does most lighting still come in the same handful of shapes? Founded in 2026, we design and print sculptural lamps that don't try to disappear into a room. They're meant to be looked at, on or off.

Every piece is home printed in small batches, then put together and wired by hand, and tested before it ships. If a shape doesn't hold up as both light and object, it doesn't make the collection.

We're just getting started. New forms are always in progress.
